Medium Voltage Switchgear

Daqo’s MV switchgear portfolio covers indoor metal-clad and withdrawable assemblies for utility, PV, industrial and commercial power distribution systems in the 7.2kV to 40.5kV range.

Product families

  • UDQ 7.2–24kV: metal-clad withdrawable switchgear.
  • UDQ-40.5: 40.5kV metal-enclosed removable switchgear for indoor single bus section systems.
  • UR4 series: higher-end medium-voltage assemblies referenced in the notebook materials alongside UDQ family positioning.

The notebook positions Zhenjiang Daqo Intelligent Electric as a specialist producer of 7.2–40.5kV medium-voltage complete equipment.

Technical strengths

  • Designed as complete power distribution equipment for control, protection and detection.
  • Support for cable and/or busbar lead-in / lead-out configurations.
  • Secondary circuits routed in dedicated trunking for cleaner installation and maintenance access.
  • Convenient breaker replacement and withdrawable architecture for servicing.
  • Standard transformer / CT accommodation and structured panel layout.
