Power Transformers

Daqo’s transformer portfolio covers dry-type, oil-immersed and pad-mounted / new energy transformer lines suited to utility, renewable, industrial and infrastructure projects in New Zealand.

Product lines

  • Dry-type transformers: low and medium voltage, energy-saving series from 10kVA to 6000kVA.
  • Oil-immersed power transformers: up to 35kV for substations, metallurgy, chemical, cement, mining, rail transit and data centres.
  • 35kV power transformers: double-winding and three-winding configurations up to 31,500kVA.
  • 10kV distribution transformers: up to 2,500kVA including SH15 amorphous alloy core series for lower loss.
  • New energy transformers: ZGS series for PV power generation, 680kVA to 4000kVA.
  • Wind power transformers: S22 Hua-style series from 4000kVA to 10,000kVA.
  • Pad-mounted transformers: three-phase liquid-filled units tested up to 34.5kV and 5000kVA.

Technical strengths

  • Direct winding on core: coils wound directly around the core to reduce gaps and improve short-circuit withstand capability.
  • Short-circuit resilience: Daqo states 3200kVA, 6600kVA and 8000kVA products passed short-circuit tests using this winding method.
  • Terahertz vacuum drying: used to remove deep moisture from insulation, reducing process time and improving insulation life.
  • Nitrogen-filled moisture protection: improves insulation performance and service life.
  • Online monitoring: optional digital data collection, wireless temperature measurement, fault warning and condition-based maintenance support.
