Key features

  • Rapid Connection Fishplate Design Horizontal busbars use bolt-free fishplate tech for quick, reliable connections. Pre-installed sliding fishplates ease on-site integration/assembly.
  • Dual Clamp Technology Vertical busbars use self-compensating dual clamps for tool-free, secure installation and stable performance under operational stress.
  • Vertical Busbar Insulation Barrier IP20 red insulation barrier marks live zones. Insulated clamps boost protection for safe maintenance.
  • Modular Frame System Standardized frames (600/700mm × 600mm W×D) enable flexible expansion via side/rear modules, facilitating easy cable routing and adaptable configuration.
  • Standard-Thickness Busbar System 5mm uniform busbars for high standardization. Supports single-bus (4000A) and double-bus (6300A) to meet diverse needs.
  • Comb-Type Busbar Clamp Specialized comb-type clamps with alloy reinforcement guarantee easy installation, high stability, and exceptional mechanical strength.
