Key features

  • Smart Operation Supports online switch status monitoring, line loss analysis, load forecasting & feeder automation.
  • Real-time Diagnostics Monitors pole switch mechanical performance, breaker lifetime alert, phase sequence & open-circuit detection.
  • Modular Compatibility Standardized modular design for flexible configuration, compatible with electromagnetic & electronic types, built-in standard encrypted bluetooth maintenance module.
  • Multi-channel Communication 2 Ethernet & 2 serial ports, supports 2G/3G/4G adaptive wireless (5G upgradable), compliant with IEC 60870-5-101/104 protocols.
