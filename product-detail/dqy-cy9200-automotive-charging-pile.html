Key features

  • Long Life & Low Standby Exceptionally long service life with a standby power consumption as low as 5W.
  • Smart Monitoring Multi-color LED indicators; supports remote startup and real-time data monitoring.
  • High Accuracy Charging High metering accuracy of up to Active Power Class 1; supports orderly charging and intelligent power adjustment.
  • Flexible Installation Flexible wall-mounted or floor-standing options for easy assembly, installation, and maintenance.
  • APP-Enabled & Durable APP functionality for station locating, charging scheduling, and fast charging; constructed with high-quality, heat-resistant PBT plastic.
  • Comprehensive Safety Comprehensive safety with nine protection layers, an emergency stop switch, and an IP54 rating with UV protection for harsh environments.
