Key features

  • Metal-Clad Safety Design The metal-clad enclosure effectively prevents fault propagation, ensuring operational safety and reliability.
  • High–Low Voltage Isolation Independent compartments provide complete isolation between high- and low-voltage sections for enhanced safety.
  • Dual-Side Maintenance Compact design with dual-side access allows convenient maintenance and space-efficient installation.
  • Modular Circuit Breaker Structure Modularized breaker units simplify structure, improve reliability, and extend service life.
  • Five-Protection Interlocking System Comprehensive interlock design prevents misoperation and enables safe closed-door operation.
  • Internal Arcing Fault Resistance Fully arc-resistant design withstands internal faults up to 40kA/1s, ensuring equipment and personnel safety.
  • Certified Compliance Manufactured in accordance with both Chinese national and international standards, having passed complete type tests.
  • Flexible Switch Options Compatible with various main switches, including vacuum circuit breakers, vacuum contactors, and load switch trucks.
  • Motorized Operation Capability Equipped with motorized modules for remote operation of circuit breakers and grounding switches.
  • Integrated Intelligent Monitoring Supports real-time monitoring of mechanical performance, temperature, partial discharge; automatic data collection/analysis to optimize power management and lifecycle performance.
