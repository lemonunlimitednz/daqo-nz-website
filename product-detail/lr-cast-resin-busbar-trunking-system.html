Key features

  • Manufacturing Process Equipped with German vacuum-casting and mixing technology, enabling digitally controlled continuous metering, homogeneous blending, and vacuum mixing of raw materials.
  • Production Formula The epoxy resin and curing agent mixture provides low shrinkage, high fluidity, easy curing, strong adhesion, and short solidification time.
  • Busbar Connection Uses monobloc torque-bolt integrated connectors for simple, time-saving, and reliable installation.
  • Waterproof Design With IP68 protection, the system prevents dust ingress and ensures long-term reliable operation even under continuous submersion.
  • Fireproof Design The LR series busbar can prevent flame propagation without requiring additional fire-barrier modules.
  • Anti-Corrosion Design The cured synthetic resin provides excellent resistance to alkalis, acids, solvents, and most molds, making it suitable for extreme environments.
  • Explosion-Proof Design The epoxy-cast busway system features a highly sealed structure that prevents flammable gases from coming into contact with live conductors.
