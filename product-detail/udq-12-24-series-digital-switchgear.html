Key features

  • Wide Brand Compatibility Compatible with major circuit breaker brands.
  • Enhanced Environmental Adaptability Suitable for coastal and chemical environments with AG5 seismic compliance.
  • High Current Capacity 12kV withdrawable units support up to 5000A; fixed units up to 6300A.
  • Superior Arc Resistance Achieves internal arc fault level IAC AFLR 50kA/1s.
  • International Certifications Certified by TÜV and GOST, compliant with 60Hz operating standards.
  • Marine Environmental Certification Approved by marine classification societies for environmental protection.
  • Smart Factory Integration Enables intelligent monitoring, fault warnings, and health assessment, meeting NB/T 42044 standards.
  • Extended Qualification Range 17.5kV and 24kV versions certified for 60Hz operation and TÜV approval.
