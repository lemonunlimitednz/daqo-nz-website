Key features

  • Lightweight Corrosion-Resistant Constructed from high-quality aluminum–magnesium alloy profiles, the enclosure is lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and low in magnetic loss with a clean, modern finish.
  • Halogen-Free Heat-Resistant The insulating materials are halogen-free thermoplastic PA6 (nylon) and thermoset DMC, providing Class B (130 °C) heat resistance and excellent mechanical and electrical properties.
  • Real-Time Monitoring & Alerts The feeder and plug-in boxes are equipped with multifunction meters that enable real-time monitoring of the busway and its downstream power consumption, while also providing fault alarms and related alerts.
