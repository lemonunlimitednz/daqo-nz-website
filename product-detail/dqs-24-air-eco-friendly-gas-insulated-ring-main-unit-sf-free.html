Key features

  • KEMA Certified Come with comprehensive KEMA technical certification, meeting rigorous global quality and safety standards.
  • Sealed & Resilient Sealed high-voltage parts (clean air insulation) resist external interference. Vacuum interrupters extinguish arcs, breakers enable early fault interruption. Optional pressure relief for lifelong maintenance-free safety.
  • Space-Saving Compact Structure Boasts a highly compact design, making it ideal for installation in space-constrained areas.
  • Durable, High-Strength Gas Tank 3mm 304 stainless steel, German TRUMPF laser-crafted, withstands 0.24MPa pressure. Ensures internal arcing safety, airtightness and aesthetics.
  • Ultra-Leakproof, Long-Lasting Performance German SEILER equipment ensures ≤0.1% annual leak rate and 99.99% pure eco-friendly gas, supporting a 40-year service life.
  • Flexible Modular Expansion Modular design allows single and common-tank combinations with side and top expansion options, extended busbars are fully enclosed and grounded for safety.
  • Misoperation Prevention Integrated mechanical and electrical interlocking eliminates faults caused by incorrect operations.
  • Long Mechanical Lifespan Vacuum circuit breakers (10,000 times), load switches (5,000 times), three-position switches (3,000 times) boast robust durability, grounding switches handle 5 short-circuit closures.
  • Smart Monitoring & Automation Advanced monitoring (temperature, density, current and voltage) and automation enable unattended operation and substation "five remote functions".
  • Consistent Quality Assurance Clean workshops, fully automated assembly lines, and factory inspection, testing equipment ensure product consistency and quality.
