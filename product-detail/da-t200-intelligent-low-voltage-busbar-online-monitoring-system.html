Key features

  • Dual Condition Monitoring Online monitoring of busbar temperature (-40°C to +200°C) and water intrusion.
  • Smart Thermal Alerts Real-time monitoring with configurable high-temperature warnings and alarms.
  • 5-Channel Multi-Sensing Sensors support up to 5 simultaneous temperature and water intrusion channels.
  • Real-time Data Analytics Management via touchscreen HMI and backend software with real-time analysis, mobile app optional.
