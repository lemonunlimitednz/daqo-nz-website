Key features

  • Compact & Flexible Design Small footprint with easy installation and high adaptability.
  • Fully Insulated & Sealed Safe, reliable, and maintenance-free structure.
  • Low Noise, Low Loss Core Robotically stacked 45° mitered joint core reduces losses and noise.
  • High-Performance HV Coil Automatic winding with E-class adhesive insulation ensures conductivity and durability.
  • Efficient LV Coil Foil winding with argon-protected welding and F-class insulation improves electrical strength.
  • Reliable Robotic Welding Ensures precision and long-term durability.
  • Advanced Coating System Robotic painting guarantees uniform film, strong adhesion, and corrosion resistance.
  • Integrated Coil Winding Both high- and low-voltage coils are wound directly on the core column for stability.
