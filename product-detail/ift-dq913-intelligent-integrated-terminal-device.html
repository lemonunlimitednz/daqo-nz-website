Key features

  • High-Performance Processing Meets high concurrency, large storage, multi-object acquisition, localized decision-making; functions including data acquisition, equipment monitoring, energy metering.
  • AI-Powered Detection Supports AI video/image analysis, load forecasting; detects fire, smoke, intrusion, predicts capacity.
  • Coordinated Management Exchanges info with medium-voltage terminals, enables hierarchical control for distribution areas, distributed generation, storage, EV charging.
  • Data Integration Integrates distribution and consumption data and services to achieve outage maintenance planning, refined line loss management, and visualization of power supply capacity.
