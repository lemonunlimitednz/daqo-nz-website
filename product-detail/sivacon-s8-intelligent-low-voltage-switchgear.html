Key features

  • Ultra-High System Safety Adopts type-tested standardized modules (TTA).
  • Ultra-High Personnel Safety Adopts arc-resistant door lock system.
  • Flexible Busbar Position Busbars can be installed at the top or rear.
  • Versatile Installation Modes Different types of installation modes can be applied in the same cabinet.
  • Adjustable Internal Separation Internal separation forms can be flexibly adjusted according to specific requirements.
  • Convenient Hinge Sesign Uses universal hinges to facilitate the conversion of door opening direction (left/right) in the future.
  • Efficient Ventilation System Features high-efficiency ventilation and easy maintenance.
  • Flexible Cable & Busbar Connection Cables and busbars can be connected from the top, bottom or rear.
