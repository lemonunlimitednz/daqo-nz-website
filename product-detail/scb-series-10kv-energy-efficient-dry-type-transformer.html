Key features

  • High Mechanical Strength The vacuum-cast coil uses premium flame-retardant Class H epoxy resin, featuring a smooth finish.
  • Moisture Resistance High mechanical strength, and excellent short-circuit resistance, operates reliably under 100% humidity.
  • Low Noise & Reduced No-Load Loss The core is processed via an imported fully automated shearing and stacking line, reducing transformer noise by 5 dB and no-load loss by 5% compared to traditional lamination.
  • Easy Commissioning & Reliable Operation No preheating is required before startup, and dehumidification is unnecessary during intermittent operation.
  • Compact, Durable Design Features a compact structure, lightweight build, crack-resistant surface, and low partial discharge for long-term reliability.
  • Maintenance-Free & Efficient Cooling Supports 150% rated load under forced-air cooling with excellent heat dissipation and long service life.
  • Safe & Eco-Friendly Flame-retardant, explosion-proof, and pollution-free; suitable for direct installation at load centers.
  • Energy-Saving Performance Low no-load and load losses ensure outstanding energy efficiency.
