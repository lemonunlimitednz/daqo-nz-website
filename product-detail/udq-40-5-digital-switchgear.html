Key features

  • Wide Compatibility Supports leading circuit breaker brands.
  • High Electrical Capacity Withdrawable switchgear rated up to 4000A with 40kA breaking capacity.
  • Arc Fault Certification Successfully passed IAC AFLR 31.5kA/1s type test for arc resistance.
  • TÜV Certified Meets 60Hz operational requirements under German TÜV standards.
  • Anti-Accident Design Conforms to State Grid "18 Anti-Accident Measures" with ≥300mm insulation spacing and no insulation boards inside.
  • High-Altitude Insulation Performance Low partial discharge (<3pC) and long creepage distances ensure operation up to 3000m.
  • Enhanced Insulation Strength Stainless-steel reinforced bushings eliminate corona discharge and reduce insulation aging.
  • Smart Monitoring System Integrates digital data acquisition, temperature and status monitoring, and health assessment functions.
