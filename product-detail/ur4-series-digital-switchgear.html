Key features

  • Certified Reliability ASTA, GOST, and NB/T 42044 certified for intelligent switchgear performance.
  • Precision Assembly Cubicle installed with pneumatic tools ensure high precision and interchangeability.
  • Comprehensive Five-Proof Interlocking Prevents misoperation with door-closed and lockable operation functions.
  • High Rated Current Withdrawable switchgear supports up to 5000A (50kA dynamic peak: 137kA).
  • Strong Breaking Capacity Fixed type up to 6300A, 63kA breaking capacity.
  • Superior Safety Performance Internal arc fault level (AFLR) of 50 kA / 1s guarantees operator protection.
  • Smart & Widely Applicable The product has passed the NB/T 42044 intelligent switchgear type test and is widely used in power grids, petrochemical plants, power plants, and other projects.
  • High-Altitude Compatible Used in power grids, petrochemical, and industrial facilities up to 3000 m altitude.
  • Robust Environmental Resistance The product has achieved tests for condensation, salt spray, and contamination.
