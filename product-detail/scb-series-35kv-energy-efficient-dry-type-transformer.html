Key features

  • High-Quality Resin Casting Vacuum-cast with Class H flame-retardant epoxy resin for smooth finish and superior mechanical strength.
  • Robust Short-Circuit Resistance Performs reliably at 100% humidity with excellent moisture protection.
  • Noise & Loss Reduction Automated core stacking reduces noise by 5 dB and no-load losses by 5% compared with manual processes.
  • No Preheating Required Ensures easy, dehumidification-free operation and reliable startup.
  • Compact & Crack-Resistant Design Small, lightweight, and durable, featuring low partial discharge and no surface crazing.
  • High Electrical Strength Provides high insulation and superior impulse withstand performance.
  • Maintenance-Free & Flame-Retardant Operates at 150% rated load under forced-air cooling; safe for direct load-center installation.
  • On-Load Tap Changer Equipped for automatic voltage regulation during load operation.
  • Energy-Saving Operation Low no-load and load losses deliver high efficiency and long-term energy savings.
