Key features

  • Robust & Safe Design Stainless steel-enclosed live components use nitrogen insulation, environment-resistant. Vacuum switching; breakers replace load switch-fuse for early fault disconnection. Optional pressure relief: safer, lifelong maintenance-free.
  • Compact Modular Design Optimized layout allows flexible configuration while maintaining a small footprint suitable for restricted spaces.
  • Robust Tank Construction Manufactured from 3mm 304 stainless steel using advanced TRUMPF laser cutting and welding technology, the tank withstands 0.3 MPa pressure and offers excellent gas tightness and aesthetics.
  • Ultra-Low Leak Rate Tested and filled using SEILER helium detection systems, ensuring a leak rate below 0.01% per year with 99.99% nitrogen purity and a 40-year service life.
  • Flexible Modular Expansion Supports single or common gas compartment configurations, with side or top expansion options. The fully enclosed, grounded expansion busbar guarantees operational safety.
  • Comprehensive Interlock Protection Mechanical and electrical interlocks work in tandem to prevent misoperation and ensure safe operation.
  • Long Mechanical Lifespan Vacuum interrupters achieve 10,000 operations, while three-position switches reach 3,000 operations. Grounding switches handle unlimited short-circuit closings.
  • Smart Monitoring and Quality Assurance Equipped with temperature rise, density, and current-voltage monitoring for unmanned substation operation, automated assembly ensures consistency and reliability.
  • Quality & Consistency Cleanroom facilities, fully automated assembly lines, and factory inspection and testing equipment ensure product consistency and quality.
