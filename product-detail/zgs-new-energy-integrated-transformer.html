Key features

  • Compact Integrated Design Combines transformer, load switch, and high-voltage fuse in one enclosure, minimizing footprint.
  • Fully Sealed & Insulated Structure Enhances safety and prevents electric shock risks.
  • Low Loss & Low Noise Core Robotically stacked cores reduce no-load loss, current, and operational noise.
  • High-Performance HV Coil Wound on an automatic machine using high-density DPE paper insulation for improved conductivity and short-circuit strength.
  • Durable LV Coil Construction Automatic foil winding with argon-protected internal welding ensures low resistance and high mechanical integrity.
  • Robotic Welding Quality Oil tank welded by robots for precision and reliability.
  • Advanced Coating System Robotic painting ensures even film thickness, strong adhesion, and corrosion resistance for harsh environments.
  • Direct Core Column Winding Both high-voltage and low-voltage coils are wound directly on the core for superior stability.
