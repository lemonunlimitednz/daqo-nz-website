Key features

  • Hermetically Sealed Design All high-voltage components are enclosed within a stainless steel gas compartment, ensuring insulation integrity and maintenance-free operation.
  • Compact Construction Ideal for confined spaces such as wind turbine towers or container substations.
  • Advanced Welding Technology 3mm 304 stainless steel tank welded with TRUMPF five-axis laser system delivers exceptional airtightness and aesthetics.
  • Helium Leak Detection Assurance SEILER automated testing guarantees a leakage rate below 0.01% per year and a service life exceeding 30 years.
  • Modular Configuration Flexible module arrangement supports diverse distribution schemes and system expansion.
  • Fully Insulated Busbars Extended busbars are insulated and shielded to enhance reliability and safety.
  • Misoperation Prevention Mechanical interlocking system provides simple and effective safety assurance.
  • Durable Mechanisms Load switch lifespan reaches 5,000 operations; vacuum circuit breaker up to 10,000 operations.
  • High Transfer Capability Load switch-fuse combination supports transfer current of 840A.
  • Intelligent Automation Options Factory-automated solutions enable intelligent switching and consistent product quality.
