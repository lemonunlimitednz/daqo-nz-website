Key features

  • Rapid Connection Fishplate Design Horizontal busbars use bolt-free fishplate tech for quick, reliable connections. Pre-installed sliding fishplates simplify on-site integration/assembly.
  • Dual Clamp Technology Vertical busbars with self-compensating dual clamps: tool-free secure installation, stable performance under stress.
  • Direct Busbar Insulation Barrier IP20 red insulation barrier marks live zones. Insulated clamps boost protection for safe maintenance.
  • Detachable Cabinet Top Beam Removable top beams facilitate efficient cabinet alignment and integration on-site, reducing assembly time.
  • Unified Busbar Specification The standardized 10×40 busbar dimension achieves up to 7300A with dual sets, ensuring easy scalability and current rating consistency.
  • Integrated Transport Base Pre-installed transport bases simplify handling, ensuring stable, efficient logistics and installation.
