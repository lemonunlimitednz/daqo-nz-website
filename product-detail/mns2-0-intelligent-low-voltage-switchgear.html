Key features

  • Arc Resistance and Structural Safety Fully type-tested for arc resistance, seismic stability & vibration resilience. Robust maintenance-free design ensures safety in harsh conditions, minimizing arc fault risks.
  • Reduced Downtime and Enhanced Reliability Withdrawable modules enable drawer replacement without power cut. Integrated intelligent control and protection units provide fault analysis, reducing production losses.
  • Predictive Maintenance and Remote Monitoring Built-in temperature monitoring enables predictive maintenance, real-time status visualization, and cloud analytics, boosting operational continuity and cutting maintenance costs.
