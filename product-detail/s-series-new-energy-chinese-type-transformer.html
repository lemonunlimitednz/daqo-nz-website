Key features

  • Optimized for PV Systems Designed for photovoltaic applications with a compact, automated configuration.
  • Efficient Heat Dissipation External radiator and oil conservator ensure temperature stability and extended service life.
  • Safe Busbar Connection High-voltage switch connected to transformer via busbar for enhanced operational safety.
  • Low Loss & Low Noise Core Automatic core stacking reduces losses, current, and noise.
  • High-Strength HV Coil Wound using automatic machinery with DPE glue-coated paper insulation.
  • Low-Resistance LV Coil Automatic foil winding with argon-protected welding ensures excellent electrical performance.
  • Reliable Robotic Welding Robotically welded tank guarantees consistent quality.
  • Corrosion-Resistant Finish Robotic painting provides uniform thickness and strong adhesion.
  • Compact Coil Configuration High and low-voltage coils wound together for improved efficiency.
