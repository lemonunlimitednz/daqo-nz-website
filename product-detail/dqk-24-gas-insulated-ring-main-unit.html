Key features

  • Fully Enclosed Gas Chamber Design All high-voltage parts are sealed in a stainless-steel chamber, ensuring environmental resilience, safety, and maintenance-free operation.
  • Compact & Durable Construction Made from 3mm 304 stainless steel and welded with TRUMPF's 3D laser system for exceptional strength and sealing.
  • High Airtightness and Long Life SEILER leak detection ensures <0.01% annual leak rate with a 30-year lifespan.
  • Insulated and Shielded Busbars Fully shielded extended busbars provide excellent reliability and protection.
  • Error-Proof Interlocking Simple interlocking mechanisms effectively prevent operational errors.
  • High Durability Mechanisms 5,000 load switch operations and 10,000 circuit breaker operations ensure longevity.
  • Advanced Load Switch-Fuse Design Handles 24kV, 1400A current transfers safely and efficiently.
  • Automated Manufacturing Factory automation supports intelligent switching and ensures consistent quality.
  • Automated Assembly Full-automatic assembly line ensures product consistency.
