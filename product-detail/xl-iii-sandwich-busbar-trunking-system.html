Key features

  • Riveting Technology Using Henrob self-piercing riveting technology ensures stable, durable joints and consistent quality, while reducing assembly cycle time.
  • Insulation Configuration Utilizes high-performance insulation materials from DuPont and 3M, providing excellent flame retardancy and full RoHS compliance.
  • Plug-in Device Patented “T”-type plug-in connectors provide reliable contact, excellent mechanical stability, and strong current-carrying capacity.
