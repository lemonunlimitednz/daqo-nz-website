Key features

  • High-Precision Measurement Equipped with high-performance processors and high-precision A/D sampling components, supporting up to 128 samples per cycle for precise measurement and reliable protection.
  • Multi-Communication Modes Supports RS485, fiber-optic ring, Ethernet, Wi-Fi, and 4G communication modes.
  • Versatile Protocol Support Integrated with a communication manager supporting protocols such as IEC 60870-5-101, IEC 60870-5-103/104, Modbus, and IEC 61850.
  • High-Voltage Compatibility Direct high-voltage sampling (up to AC 1000V) with support for high-voltage side tripping circuits and three-circuit differential protection.
