Intelligent Low Voltage Systems

Daqo’s low-voltage portfolio centers on modular intelligent switchgear families designed for distribution centres, motor control centres and lighting control systems in industrial, infrastructure and commercial environments.

Product families

  • MODAN6000: flagship modular intelligent LV system originating from Moeller technology; structured for 30+ year service life.
  • MODAQO-M: independently developed modular LV system with flexible design, safe operation and economical configuration.
  • Both families can serve as distribution centres, motor control centres and power / lighting control systems.
  • Functional sections include fixed, withdrawable and fixed partition arrangements in one cabinet lineup.

Technical strengths

  • Integrated frame structure: seamless square steel tubes with alloy connectors for high mechanical strength and transport resilience.
  • C-profile busbar system: high current carrying capability, high mechanical strength, good heat dissipation and non-porous connections.
  • K-board drawer contacts: beryllium copper alloy and silver elastic laminations for reliable electrical contact and durable drawer insertion/removal.
  • Flexible installation: cable or busbar incoming / outgoing from top or bottom; mixed unit types in the same lineup.
  • Live replacement support: drawer units can be replaced while energized with front protection up to IP20 when withdrawn.

Standards & certifications

  • Designed to IEC 61439-2:2011 and IEC 61641.
  • Notebook references ASTA, CE and CB certifications, plus full type-tested assemblies.
  • Passed seismic tests, simulated transport tests and arc tests.
  • Suitable for projects needing anti-earthquake, anti-vibration and anti-shock performance.

Best-fit NZ applications

  • Power and energy projects
  • Petrochemical and mining facilities
  • Commercial buildings and municipal projects
  • Data centres and communications / electronics sites
  • Rail transit and water treatment systems

The notebook also highlights intelligent upgrades: sensors, power monitoring, energy management, remote loop control and app-based lifecycle visibility — useful for modern digital operations in NZ infrastructure.
