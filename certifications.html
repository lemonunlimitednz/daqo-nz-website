Certifications page

Certifications & Standards

Daqo’s product lines and manufacturing systems are backed by internationally relevant standards, third-party testing and management system certifications that matter for New Zealand utility, industrial and infrastructure procurement.

Management systems

  • ISO 9001
  • ISO 14001
  • ISO 45001 / OHSAS18001 references across business units

Notebook sources show these systems across transformer, MV and LV manufacturing entities.

Product certifications

  • ASTA
  • TÜV
  • UL
  • CE
  • CB
  • CCC and other referenced certifications for certain domestic product lines

Relevant standards

  • Transformers: IEC 60076, IEEE C57, EN references
  • MV switchgear: IEC 62271-200, IEC 62271-1, IEC 62271-100, GB3906, GB/T11022 and related standards
  • LV systems: IEC 61439-2:2011, IEC 61641, GB/T7251.12-2023

Type testing & resilience

  • Type-tested assemblies (TTA) referenced for LV systems
  • Seismic testing / IE-level anti-earthquake performance on LV switchgear families
  • Arc testing and anti-arc designs
  • Simulated transport tests
  • Short-circuit testing on transformer models
  • Applications suitable for seaside / salt-spray / condensation conditions on MV materials
