About Daqo Group

Founded in 1965, Daqo Group is a large-scale manufacturer of intelligent electrical equipment, power distribution systems and renewable energy solutions with major manufacturing, R&D and project delivery capability relevant to New Zealand infrastructure buyers.

Group scale

Daqo is positioned as a global manufacturer in electrical, new energy, power electronics and components. Notebook materials reference annual turnover around USD 8.5 billion, more than 17,000 employees, 6 production bases, multiple research institutes and a broad manufacturing company network.

Manufacturing footprint

Notebook sources describe a digitally enabled manufacturing ecosystem including robotic assembly, automated production lines and specialized manufacturing methods such as terahertz vacuum drying for transformer insulation quality and advanced modular switchgear production.

Relevant business divisions

  • Zhenjiang Daqo Power Transformer Co., Ltd. — dry-type, oil-immersed and pad-mounted transformers.
  • Zhenjiang Daqo Intelligent Electric Co., Ltd. — 7.2kV–40.5kV medium-voltage switchgear.
  • Zhenjiang Klockner-Moeller Electrical Systems Co., Ltd. — modular low-voltage distribution and MCC systems.

NZ market positioning

  • Utility and urban power distribution
  • Renewables and battery energy storage systems
  • Heavy industry and mining
  • Data centres and critical infrastructure
  • Commercial and municipal electrical projects

Notebook sources also indicate New Zealand in Daqo’s export network and Oceania as an early international market region.
