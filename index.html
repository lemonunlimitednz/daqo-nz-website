Company background

Product portfolio

Professional electrical solutions across transformer, MV and LV systems.

The NZ-facing proposition is strongest where proven hardware, industrial durability and standards-based designs are required across power conversion, protection and distribution.

Power Transformers

Dry-type, oil-immersed and padmount transformer solutions for industrial, utility and renewable use cases. Offerings include energy-saving dry-type units and oil-immersed power transformers for high-load, high-reliability environments.

Medium Voltage Switchgear

7.2–40.5kV switchgear systems including UR4 and UDQ series, designed around safety interlocks, enclosed metal-clad construction and digital condition monitoring capabilities.

Low Voltage Distribution

Modular intelligent low-voltage systems such as MODAN6000 and MODAQO-M for distribution centres, motor control centres and lighting control — with energy management integration and long service life.
