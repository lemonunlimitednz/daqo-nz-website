Company profile

Established industrial scale with global delivery experience.

Founded in 1965, Daqo Group is a large-scale electrical equipment manufacturer with strengths in power distribution, renewable energy and intelligent electrical systems. The group has operated internationally for over two decades and lists New Zealand among its served markets.

Why this matters for New Zealand

NZ buyers typically need proven compliance, long-life equipment, harsh-environment suitability and credible manufacturing capacity. Daqo’s portfolio is positioned around exactly those requirements: IEC-compliant systems, heavy-duty industrial application, digital monitoring and scalable delivery.

Product portfolio

Professional solutions across transformer, MV and LV infrastructure.

Power Transformers

Dry-type, oil-immersed and padmount transformer solutions engineered for industrial, utility and renewable applications. Offerings include energy-saving dry-type units from 10kVA to 6000kVA and oil-immersed power transformers used in high-load, high-reliability environments.

Medium Voltage Switchgear

UR4, UDQ and related 7.2–40.5kV switchgear systems designed for safety, serviceability and digital monitoring. Configurations support high current and short-circuit requirements with metal-clad enclosed designs and intelligent maintenance functions.

Low Voltage Distribution Systems

Modular intelligent LV distribution platforms such as MODAN6000 and MODAQO-M for distribution centres, motor control centres and lighting control systems, with built-in energy management and long asset life.
