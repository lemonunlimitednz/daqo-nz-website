Company Background

Daqo Group Co., Ltd. (founded in 1965) is a state-level, large-scale enterprise conglomerate and one of China's top ten leading electrical industry companies. We specialize in intelligent electrical equipment, transportation systems, and renewable energy solutions.

  • Scale: Over 17,000 employees globally, with total assets exceeding $8.3 Billion USD.
  • Infrastructure: We operate 6 major production bases, 4 research institutes, and 23+ manufacturing subsidiaries.
  • Global Reach: Our products have a proven track record, operating safely in international markets since 2003, supported by a network of global distributors.

Key Product Offerings

Our R&D and manufacturing capabilities are at the forefront of the industry, offering a comprehensive suite of solutions tailored for demanding industrial and commercial applications.

Power Transformers

High-reliability transformers designed for heavy load environments. We offer Padmount, Dry-Type (10kVA - 6000kVA), and Oil-Immersed Transformers (≤35kV). Our advanced manufacturing includes direct winding on core and terahertz vacuum drying for superior performance.

Medium Voltage Switchgear

Producing over 10,000 sets annually, our MV switchgear (7.2~40.5kV) includes fully metal-clad and enclosed designs (UR4 and UDQ series). They feature robust mechanical safety interlocks and smart, condition-based maintenance capabilities.

Low Voltage Systems

The MODAN6000 and MODAQO-M modular intelligent low-voltage power distribution systems serve as distribution centers, motor control centers, and lighting control systems. Built for durability (30+ year lifespan) with smart energy management integration.

Target Industries in New Zealand

Daqo’s durable, high-capacity equipment is engineered for New Zealand's critical infrastructure and industrial sectors:
